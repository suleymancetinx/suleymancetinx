### 💫 About Me
I am a **Front-End Developer** and **UI/UX Designer** based in Turkiye. I specialize in building highly accessible, mobile-first, and user-centric web applications. My development philosophy revolves around reducing cognitive load through clean interfaces and solving complex UX problems with simple, effective, and modern code.

- 🔭 **Currently working on:** High-accessibility UX tools and modern single-file web apps (*Menasik Assistant, KermesKasa, HitAPP*).
- 🌱 **Currently learning:** Advanced Vanilla JS patterns & CSS-based UI animations.
- 💬 **Ask me about:** Vanilla JS, UI/UX Design, Accessibility, and Front-End Architecture.
- 📫 **Contact:** You can reach me through my [website](https://suleymancetin.com.tr) or LinkedIn.
